My idea is an AI powered instagram and tiktok slide campaign automator. basically, you create your template layout for slides (which determins the format like "harsh truthes", "5 things that helped me X with numerated slides", then prompt the internal AI with the content style, and topic. It generates the content based on the theme and niche, as well as your template. Then it creates the slides and auto posts them for the allotted time slots you choose. 

This solves a personal challenge where I have been using Ai to mass script out 7 slides a day, which I manually choose images for and copy paste the slide text. 

This essentially puts it all in one. 

Through my manual system, I also started doing an A/B testing of the variety of styles I had the AI write me.

The problem I faced was that keeping track of all these formats was complicated, and the UX of doing this all in an AI chat sucks. 

So my post MVP plan is to add A/B testing between templates, so you can see analytics for posts, compare the overall for two or more template styles and run these tests for a week or two automatically, then take the winner and scrap the less successful ones and move on to testing more (or running a mixed campaign with a few variants at different times)

I am obsessed with the idea of extreme and automated optimization of social media posts and want to be able to grow multiple accounts asynchronously while they all "learn" the best format for their niche.
