How to use this for Different Styles (The "Plug-ins")
You can create a "Library" of these objects. When you want a specific "feel," you tell the AI: "Use Template T-002 (The Identity Magnet)."

1. For "High Saves" (The Information Bomb)
Style: Reference Sheet or Protocol.

Logic: Dense info that is too much to memorize in one watch. Users must save it to use it later.

Structure: "The 5-Step Morning Script for RAS Priming."

2. For "High Followers" (The Identity Magnet)
Style: Manifesto or "Us vs. Them."

Logic: Defines the viewer's identity. If they agree, they follow because your account "represents" them.

Structure: "Why 99% of people fail at manifestation (and the 1% shift we use instead)."

3. For "High Likes/Engagement" (The Relatability/Entertainment)
Style: Before vs. After or "POV."

Logic: Low friction, high emotional resonance.

Structure: "POV: You finally stopped overthinking and started scripting your reality."



# The "A/B Testing" Prompt Strategy
Once you have these JSON templates, your prompting becomes a scientific experiment. You would prompt your generator like this:

"Generate content for [Niche] using Template T-001 (Saves) and Template T-002 (Follows) for the same topic.

Topic: Commanding respect in a room.

For T-001: Give me a dense 5-slide protocol on somatic grounding.

For T-002: Give me a 3-slide manifesto on the 'Inner Authority' identity."

How to Analyze the Results
By keeping these JSONs in a folder (or a simple spreadsheet), you can track your TikTok analytics:

If T-001 has 10x more saves than T-002: You know your audience wants Utility. Double down on "The Protocol" style.

If T-002 has a higher follow rate: Your audience is looking for Community/Identity. Start posting more Manifesto
